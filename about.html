Afro Revolution
Top Musical Concert TO witness

Adekunle Gold – Sensational singer, Adekunle Gold will be treating his fans to beautiful pieces of music at his ‘Catch Me If You Can’ concert in Lagos.

On the same night, fans of “Buga” and “Odo” will be treated to some good music by Vadoo, Kizz Daniel. The sensational artist who has had a breakout year culminating in a global tour in major cities around the world and a performance at the opening of the FIFA World Cup in Qatar is set to bring the Afroclassic experience to fans and the audience in Nigeria.

Lagos will host one of the biggest artists in the country at the 2022 Afro Revolution. Performing artistes include Kizz Daniel, Flavour, Fireboy DML, Portable, Terry G, Oritsefemi, Durella, Mr Real, Candybleakz, Ladé, Jumabee, Tolibian
